Delete Your Maestro Account

This guide outlines how to permanently delete your Maestro account and associated data.

Steps to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Maestro app on your device.
  2. Go to the Settings section.
  3. Select "Delete Account".
  4. Confirm your action when prompted.
Important: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, including invoices and preferences, will be permanently deleted.

What Happens Next?

  • Your personal data will be completely removed from our servers.
  • The deletion is processed instantly after confirmation.
